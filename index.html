How to Play

  • 🧑‍🚀 Crewmates complete tasks around the map.
  • 🔪 Impostors secretly eliminate crewmates.
  • 🚨 Press Report near a body or Emergency at the table to call a meeting.
  • 🗳️ Vote to eject the player you think is the impostor.
  • ✅ Crewmates win by completing all tasks OR ejecting all impostors.
  • 💀 Impostors win by eliminating enough crewmates.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys — Move
  • E / Space — Interact / Use / Kill / Report
  • Mobile: on-screen joystick + action button
